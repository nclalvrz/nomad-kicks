About Us — Nomad Kicks

Welcome to Nomad Kicks, your trusted destination for affordable, stylish, and high-quality footwear and streetwear essentials. We are built for individuals who move with confidence—whether in sport, in lifestyle, or in everyday hustle. Our mission is simple: to bring premium-looking kicks and fashion-forward pieces at prices that stay accessible for everyone.

At Nomad Kicks, we specialize in athletic footwear, streetwear, and rare sneaker-inspired styles that combine comfort, durability, and modern design. Every item in our collection is carefully selected to help you stay on trend without compromising quality or breaking your budget. We believe that great style should never be limited by price.

As a proudly local footwear store, we are known for our unbeatable value deals such as 3 for ₱999, Buy 1 Take 1 promotions, and other affordable bundles. These offers are part of our commitment to making fashion more inclusive and practical for every customer. Quality and affordability are always at the core of what we do.

To make shopping even more convenient, Nomad Kicks operates both a physical presence and an online store, giving you the freedom to shop anytime and anywhere. With nationwide shipping across the Philippines, we make sure your next pair of kicks is always within reach—no matter your location.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, stylish, and high-quality footwear and streetwear to every Filipino by making premium fashion accessible without sacrificing comfort, durability, or authenticity.

Our Vision

To become one of the Philippines’ most trusted and recognized local footwear brands, known for combining affordability, quality, and street culture while building a strong and inclusive fashion community.

Our Goals

  • ✔ Offer consistently high-quality footwear at budget-friendly prices
  • ✔ Expand our product line to include more streetwear and exclusive sneaker styles
  • ✔ Strengthen and improve our online shopping experience and nationwide delivery
  • ✔ Build long-term customer trust and loyalty through excellent service
  • ✔ Grow Nomad Kicks into a leading name in the local footwear and streetwear industry

At Nomad Kicks, we don’t just sell shoes—we deliver style, confidence, and value with every step you take.
